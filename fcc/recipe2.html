Omelette

Easy way to make breakfast with Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter or cooking oil
  • Optional fillings:
    • 2 tablespoons shredded cheese
    • 2 tablespoons diced vegetables (onion, bell pepper, tomato, spinach, mushrooms)
    • 2 tablespoons cooked meat (ham, sausage, bacon bits)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the fillings:
    • If using vegetables or meat, cook them first in a pan until soft (2 - 3 minutes). Set aside.
  2. Beat the eggs:
    • In a bowl, crack the eggs.
    • Add milk or water, salt, and pepper.
    • Beat with spoon or whisk until well mixed and slightly frothy
  3. Heat the pan:
    • Put non stick skillet over medium heat.
    • Add the butter or oil and let it melt, coating the pan.
  4. Cook the omelette:
    • Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
    • Let it cook undisturbed for 30 - 60 seconds, then gently stir the edges with a spatula, letting uncooked egg flow to the sides.
    • When the bottom is set but the top is still slightly runny, reduce the heat to low.
  5. Add the fillings:
    • Sprinkle your fillings on one half of the omelette.
  6. Fold and finish:
    • Gently fold the omelette in half over the fillings.
    • Let it cook another 30 seconds to 1 minute until the inside is just set and cheese is melted.
  7. Serve:
    • Slide the omelette onto a plate.
    • Garnish with herbs like parsley or chives if desired.
